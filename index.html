How to Play

  1. Click the "Play" button to begin a slidle puzzle.
  2. Click a tile by the empty space and start sliding.
  3. Arrange tiles to form the complete picture.
  4. Track your progress via the timer and moves counters.
  5. Click the "Hint" button to view solved puzzle.
  6. Click Play button again to reshuffle and restart.
  7. Play and pause audio by clicking the volume icon.
  8. Ready to solve Slidles? Enjoy!

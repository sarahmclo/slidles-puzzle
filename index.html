How to Play

  1. Click the "Play" button to begin a slidle puzzle.
  2. Click a tile by the empty space and start sliding.
  3. Arrange tiles to form the complete picture.
  4. Keep an eye on the timer and moves counter to track your progress.
  5. Click the "Hint" button to view solved puzzle.
  6. Click the "Switch" button to change puzzle.
  7. Click Play button again to reshuffle and restart.
  8. Play and pause music by clicking the volume icon.
  9. Ready to solve Slidles? Enjoy!
